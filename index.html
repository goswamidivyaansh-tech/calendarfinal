How the 27 days are sequenced

Once school resumes, the ~2.5h evenings run a fixed rhythm: QR always, DM/VR alternating, an 8-minute review, and a 48-hour error-bank re-attempt woven in two days after every session; a timed mini-mock lands every ~3 days to keep real pacing honest, and both SAC-prep eves (14 & 21 July) are protected. Phase 3 converts practice into match fitness with three full mocks spaced ~3 days apart — Practice Test A on Sat 25, then the two officials, C on Tue 28 and D on Fri 31, as your most realistic dress rehearsals — with only error-bank and weakest-category work in between. The final weekend tapers hard: nothing new after Fri 31, checkpoint rehearsal and sleep on Sun 2, priming and go on Mon 3.
